Schedules of Condition

A Schedule of Condition is prepared to record the condition of a premises prior to a lease commencing, and we understand the requirement to collate this document with meticulous detail to protect the respective liabilities of investors and occupiers alike.

A Schedule of Condition may also be prepared to form part of a Party Wall Award under the Party Wall etc Act 1996 to protect Building Owners and Adjoining Owners.
