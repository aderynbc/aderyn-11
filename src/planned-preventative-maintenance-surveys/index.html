Planned Preventative Maintenance Surveys

A building and its plant and equipment represent a major investment for landlords and occupiers alike and those assets need to be appropriately maintained to operate efficiently. A well maintained property provides a safe and productive work environment whilst creating lasting value. A proactive, rather than reactive, approach to building maintenance is therefore essential.

Aderyn Building Consultancy can provide quantifiable and defined programmes of planned maintenance over periods of time to suit our respective clients requirements and budgets. Our programmes will also identify any packages of work that can be undertaken concurrently to benefit from economies of scale and limit disruption to building users.
