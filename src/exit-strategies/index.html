Exit Strategies

If you are a tenant of commercial premises and you are looking to relocate, a well planned exit strategy is likely to save you money and stress. In particular, it is important that you deal correctly with the legalities of terminating your lease and consider at an early stage your potential dilapidation liabilities. We can assist you in the development of your strategy and how best to reduce your overall financial exposure.
