Building Pathology / Defect Diagnosis

A building defect is defined as a material, component or finish that does not meet its expected performance criteria. At Aderyn Building Consultancy we use our knowledge and understanding of these issues to provide a service that goes beyond simple diagnosis by offering analysis of the remedial measures required to put the defect right as efficiently and effectively as possible.

All our reporting is presented in a clear and concise format to provide our clients with the necessary information to make informed decisions as to how best resolve the identified defect.
