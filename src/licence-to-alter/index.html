Licence to Alter

Most occupier’s leases place a requirement that landlords consent must be obtained before any alterations can be undertaken to their demise, which is commonly documented by means of a Licence to Alter.

We can provide our expertise to landlords in assessing the occupiers proposals and provide relevant advice on the potential impact of the alterations at the present time, or in the future, to ensure we protect your investment accordingly. We will also monitor the works as required and ensure any statutory documentation is obtained and provided, such as Building Regulations and Planning Permission.
