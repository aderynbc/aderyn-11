Vendor Surveys

Many large commercial deals are completed with the assistance of a full vendors survey upon which the eventual purchaser may rely. Vendor surveys are now becoming an increasingly popular tool in the transaction process.

Our experience at Aderyn Building Consultancy ensures that the client will receive a high quality and transparent vendor survey, which in turn will reduce the risk of failed sales negotiations.

The benefits of a vendor survey will highlight any major issues at the early stages of the sales process, allowing time for the vendor to attend to them, which can save considerable expenditure.
