Contract Administration

We work with property owners, occupiers, investors and developers to plan, implement and deliver their construction, alteration and refurbishment projects. Our technical knowledge and contractual understanding allows us to monitor, manage and deliver to our clients. Where appointed as Contract Administrators we will act as the client’s agent and our pre-contract services include advice on design, preparing detailed technical specifications and drawings, liaising with sub-consultants, collating and issuing the tender pack, tender analysis and reporting, and appointment of the most suitable contractor.

Post contract services include the monitoring of works on site against programme and budget, undertake valuations, certifying payments and Practical Completion.
